🙋 About Yumoria

We're Not Your
Average Cafe, Babe

Okay real talk — Yumoria started because two broke college students were TIRED of choosing between eating good and not being broke. Plot twist: you should never have to.

We built this whole thing on one philosophy: joy is not a luxury. Good food shouldn't cost half your rent. A great cafe shouldn't have dress codes. And your order shouldn't take 45 mins when you're hangry AF.

So yeah. We're the cafe that finally gets it. We get YOU. We're affordable, we're aesthetic, we're chaotic, we're wholesome, and most importantly — we're delicious. No gatekeeping. Just good food.

Come for the menu. Stay for the vibes. Leave with leftovers. That's the Yumoria promise. ✌️
